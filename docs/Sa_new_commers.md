1. Kota / Bunny Chow

Ingredients:
Quarter loaf bread, chips, polony/Russian, cheese, atchar, sauces.
Recipe:

Fry your chips till golden.

Slice polony or Russian, fry a bit for flavour.

Hollow the bread.

Layer chips → meat → cheese → atchar → sauces.

Close it, crush it.

Bunny Chow (Curry Version):
Hollow half loaf, fill with beef/chicken curry, top with gravy.

2. Pap & Vleis (With Chakalaka)

Pap:
4 cups water, 2 cups maize meal, salt.

Boil water, add salt.

Add maize meal slowly, stir till thick.

Drop heat, steam 10–15 min.

Vleis:
Any chops/wors/steak.

Season with salt, pepper, spice.

Braai or pan-grill till cooked.

Chakalaka:
Onions, peppers, carrots, beans, curry powder.

Fry onions + peppers.

Add carrots + spice.

Add baked beans.

Simmer 5 min.

3. Braai Meat

Ingredients:
Boerewors, chops, steak, chicken pieces.
Recipe:

Season meat or use marinade.

Heat charcoal till white-hot.

Cook wors low and slow, then chops, then steak.

Chicken needs lowest heat and longest time.

Rest the meat before eating.

4. Shisanyama

Ingredients:
Fresh beef cuts, chicken, wors, pap, chakalaka.
Recipe:

Buy meat from butchery (often pre-spiced).

Grill on open flame.

Serve with pap + chakalaka + coleslaw.

Eat with hands for max enjoyment.

5. KFC-Style Fried Chicken

Ingredients:
Chicken pieces, flour, paprika, garlic, onion powder, salt, pepper, eggs, oil.
Recipe:

Mix flour + spices.

Dip chicken in egg mix then flour.

Rest coated chicken 10 min.

Deep fry on medium heat till golden and cooked through.

6. Gatsby

Ingredients:
Long roll, slap chips, steak strips or polony, lettuce, tomato, sauces.
Recipe:

Cook slap chips (soft and saucy).

Fry steak strips.

Slice roll, layer lettuce → steak → chips → sauces.

Close and cut into four.

7. Vetkoek & Mince

Vetkoek:
Flour, yeast, sugar, salt, warm water.

Make dough, let rise.

Shape balls, deep fry till golden.

Mince:
Onion, mince, curry powder, veggies.

Fry onion, add mince, season.

Add carrots/peas.

Simmer till rich.

Fill vetkoeks.

8. Biryani

Ingredients:
Basmati rice, chicken/lamb, potatoes, onion, garlic, ginger, biryani spice, yoghurt.
Recipe:

Parboil rice till halfway done.

Fry onions, add meat + spices + yoghurt.

Add potatoes.

Layer rice on top, add saffron water if available.

Steam on low for 30–40 min.

9. Samoosas

Ingredients:
Ready-made samoosa pur, mince/potato filling, spices.
Recipe:

Cook mince with onions and spices.

Fold sheets into triangles, fill with mixture.

Seal edges with flour paste.

Deep fry till crispy.

10. Chicken Licken–Style Hot Wings

Ingredients:
Chicken wings, flour, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, salt, pepper.
Recipe:

Coat wings in seasoned flour.

Fry till crispy.

Toss in your own hot sauce (butter + vinegar + chili + sugar).
